#Privacy Policy for Bus ETA STM Montreal
Effective date: March 28, 2026

Bus ETA STM Montreal respects your privacy.

##Information We Collect
Bus ETA STM Montreal does not collect, store, or sell personal information.
The app does not require account creation and does not use advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

##Location Data
If you allow location access, the app uses your device location to show nearby bus arrivals and related transit information.
Your location is used only for app functionality and is not stored by us.

##Transit Data
The app uses STM open transit data, including static and realtime transit information, to provide bus arrival times, live vehicle details, and service alerts.

##Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

##Third-Party Services
The app may connect to STM data services and Apple system services only as needed to load transit information and provide core app functionality.

##Children’s Privacy
This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

##Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in a future app update or on the published policy page.
